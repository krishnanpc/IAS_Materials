% Abstract gives a brief summary of the main points of a paper:
\section*{Abstract}
  This paper gives a brief overview of the Usage of Spiking Neural Network to model Model-
Free Reinforcement Learning in order to provide a framework for solving partially observable reinforcement learning (PORL). It also presents a comparison against other used methods to show the validity of this solution.

\section{Introduction}
\label{sec:introduction}
Reinforcement learning is one of the Bio-Inspired machine learning areas of research. It has roots in both behavioral behaviorist psychology and Cognitive science \cite{Barto1998}. 
\section{Background Information and Previous Work}
\label{sec:basics}
A brief overview of the current state of the art solution used to solve the PORL problem. Previous work done to bring reinforcement learning into the scene of the problem.

\section{Methods Descriptions}
\label{sec:model}
Deep explanation of the model described in the original paper \cite{Nakano2015}, and how does it work. The realisation of the Free-energy-based reinforcement learning within it. Also it will talk about the variation of using a working memory to consider history of the environment state.

\section{Experimentation}
\label{sec:Expe}
Describing the experiments done and the comparison of it versus other methods.

\section{Conclusion}
\label{sec:concl}
A discussion of the method validity regarding the problem it's trying to solve (PORL).
